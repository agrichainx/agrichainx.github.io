Agrichainx launch ICO

We want to become decentralized platform on the B2B market for trading agricultural crops, food products, and is running as a crowdfunding project built on Blockchain technology with the idea of Smart Contracts. The aim of our project is to let the small and medium businesses in any part of the world enter the global trade markets safely, and minimize the price of food products for the end user without the quality loss. More details you can read at our ICO website - http://agrolot.io
